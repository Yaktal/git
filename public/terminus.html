Best Places To Visit In Jos

by Taylor Teacher07/24/202405/17/2025

Jos, the capital of Plateau State in Nigeria, is a vibrant city known for its cool climate, stunning landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. It offers a mix of historical sites, natural wonders, and adventure-filled locations that make it a must-visit destination. Whether you’re looking for breathtaking waterfalls, wildlife encounters, or historical landmarks, Jos has something for every traveler. With a population of over 900,000 people, the city is a melting pot of diverse cultures, traditions, and cuisines. Lets explore some of the best places you should visit in Jos and why they deserve a spot on your travel bucket list.

Jos Wildlife Park

Established in 1957, the Jos Wildlife Park is one of the largest zoological gardens in Nigeria, covering an expansive area of 8 square kilometers. It is home to various wild animals such as lions, baboons, pythons, and hippos, making it a perfect spot for wildlife lovers. Visitors can take a guided tour to explore the natural habitat of these animals while learning about conservation efforts. The park also has a beautiful picnic area where families and groups can relax after an exciting tour. With its serene environment and well-maintained facilities, this park is a must-visit for anyone in Jos.

Shere Hills

For adventure seekers, Shere Hills offers one of the best hiking and climbing experiences in Nigeria. These hills stand at about 1,829 meters above sea level, providing breathtaking panoramic views of Jos and its surroundings. The rugged terrain attracts both novice and experienced hikers who are eager to explore the region’s natural beauty. Many training exercises for the Nigerian military and paramilitary forces are conducted here due to the challenging landscape. If you love outdoor activities and thrilling experiences, this destination should be at the top of your list.

Assop Falls

Located about 64 kilometers from Jos, Assop Falls is a magnificent waterfall that cascades down rocky cliffs, creating a spectacular view. The falls are surrounded by lush greenery, making it a perfect spot for photography, relaxation, and picnics. During the rainy season, the water flow is at its peak, offering visitors a more dramatic and refreshing sight. Tourists and locals frequently visit this site for weekend getaways and to enjoy the soothing sounds of nature. If youre looking for an escape from the hustle and bustle of the city, Assop Falls is the ideal destination.

Riyom Rock Formation

The Riyom Rock Formation is one of the most iconic natural landmarks in Plateau State, located about 25 kilometers from Jos. These naturally stacked rocks create an unusual but stunning scenery that has drawn attention from geologists and tourists alike. The formation looks like a man-made sculpture, but it is purely a work of nature, shaped over millions of years. Visitors often climb the rocks to get an elevated view of the surrounding landscape. This location offers a perfect blend of nature, adventure, and photography opportunities.

National Museum Jos

The National Museum in Jos is an excellent place to learn about Nigerias rich history and cultural diversity. Established in 1952, the museum houses a vast collection of artifacts, including Nok terracotta sculptures, traditional costumes, and ancient tools. One of the highlights of the museum is the Archaeological Gallery, which provides insight into early Nigerian civilizations. Visitors can explore various exhibits that showcase traditional arts, crafts, and historical events. If you are a history enthusiast, this museum offers an educational and enlightening experience.

Solomon Lar Amusement Park

For those traveling with family, the Solomon Lar Amusement Park is an exciting place to visit in Jos. It features a variety of fun rides, recreational activities, and beautiful gardens perfect for relaxation. Children can enjoy thrilling attractions, while adults can unwind in the well-landscaped park. The serene environment and well-maintained facilities make it a great spot for picnics, social gatherings, and weekend outings. Whether you’re a local or a visitor, this park offers a delightful experience for all age groups.

Kurra Falls

Another hidden gem in Jos is Kurra Falls, a scenic waterfall located in the heart of Plateau State. The falls are formed by a hydroelectric dam that supplies power to surrounding areas, adding a unique industrial touch to its natural beauty. Visitors can take boat rides on the water, explore the rocky terrains, or simply enjoy the refreshing atmosphere. The surrounding vegetation makes this location a perfect place for birdwatching and nature walks. If you’re a lover of waterfalls, Kurra Falls should definitely be on your travel list.

Rayfield Resort

If you are looking for luxury and relaxation, Rayfield Resort is one of the top choices in Jos. The resort is situated near a natural lake, providing visitors with boating, fishing, and other water activities. It also has a well-equipped restaurant that serves both local and international dishes. Guests can enjoy a comfortable stay in beautifully designed rooms while experiencing the tranquility and scenic beauty of the area. Whether youre on a romantic getaway or a family vacation, Rayfield Resort offers a perfect blend of comfort and adventure.

Pandam Wildlife Park

Located about 100 kilometers from Jos, Pandam Wildlife Park is a vast conservation area that protects various species of animals and plants. It is home to hippos, antelopes, crocodiles, and numerous bird species, making it a paradise for nature lovers. Visitors can embark on guided safaris to explore the diverse ecosystems and learn about conservation efforts. The park also features a beautiful lake where tourists can enjoy canoeing and fishing. If you love wildlife and adventure, this park is worth the trip.

Miango Rest Home

For travelers looking for a quiet retreat, Miango Rest Home offers a peaceful escape from city life. The retreat center is located in a picturesque setting, surrounded by rolling hills and lush greenery. Visitors can enjoy hiking, meditation, and serene nature walks while soaking in the breathtaking views. The facility is well-known for its hospitality and cozy accommodations, making it an ideal spot for a relaxing getaway. Whether youre a solo traveler or with family, Miango Rest Home is a perfect place to unwind.

Top Things to Do in Jos

  1. Explore Jos Wildlife Park and see exotic animals.
  2. Hike up Shere Hills for a panoramic view of the city.
  3. Visit the National Museum to learn about Nigerian history.
  4. Relax at Rayfield Resort with lakeside activities.
  5. Experience the stunning beauty of Assop Falls.
  6. Take a guided safari at Pandam Wildlife Park.
  7. Enjoy fun rides at Solomon Lar Amusement Park.

Travel Tips for Visiting Jos

  1. Pack warm clothing as Jos has a cooler climate than most Nigerian cities.
  2. Hire a local guide for a better experience at tourist sites.
  3. Visit during the dry season for easier access to outdoor locations.
  4. Try local Plateau dishes for a unique culinary experience.
  5. Ensure you have a camera to capture the stunning landscapes.
  6. Use a trusted transportation service for safety and convenience.
  7. Respect local cultures and traditions while interacting with residents.

Pro Tip: Book accommodations in advance, especially during peak seasons, to secure the best rates and avoid last-minute hassles.

Jos is a city where nature, adventure, and culture blend seamlessly, offering visitors an unforgettable experience.

Jos is a hidden gem in Nigeria, offering breathtaking landscapes, fascinating history, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you’re an adventure seeker, a nature lover, or a history enthusiast, there’s something for everyone in this vibrant city. From the roaring waterfalls to the serene wildlife parks, Jos is a destination that promises an unforgettable journey. Plan your visit today and immerse yourself in the unique attractions this city has to offer. Don’t forget to share this guide with fellow travelers and bookmark it for your next adventure.
